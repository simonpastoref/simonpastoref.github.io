The Final Codedown

    Many of the world’s most innovative companies discover top talent through carefully crafted challenges. In December we’ll be showcasing 15 of the most exciting startups through this competition. Every day, one of the startups will be releasing a challenge, giving them visibility among a large audience of exceptional technical talent. Participants will then attempt to solve the challenges to win exciting prizes and maybe an opportunity to join one of these fast-growing companies.
